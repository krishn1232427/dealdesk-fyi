DealDesk

Purchase planning, deal review, and exact item links.

DealDesk helps shoppers review purchase candidates, calculate estimated checkout totals, compare price signals, save buying plans, watch prices, review local finds, and open exact merchant item pages when a valid purchase link is available.

View on the App Store

How it works

Review stays native. Buy goes to the merchant.

Review opens a DealDesk decision sheet with price, coupon, shipping, seller, evidence, and risk signals. Buy opens the merchant item page so the customer can confirm checkout details directly with the merchant.

Decision Tools

Save buying plans, compare candidates, watch prices, estimate checkout totals, review coupon status, and export decision summaries.

Affiliate Disclosure

DealDesk may earn a commission from some purchases when users choose to open merchant purchase links. The app shows this disclosure near Buy actions.

Customer Control

The merchant cart remains the source of truth for final price, taxes, shipping, availability, return terms, and checkout conditions.
