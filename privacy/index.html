Privacy Policy

DealDesk uses limited product telemetry and disclosed purchase links.

Last updated: July 2, 2026

No account required.

DealDesk does not require a Launchdesk account, does not include advertising SDKs, does not sell user data, and does not use telemetry for tracking.

Product Telemetry

DealDesk sends limited product telemetry directly to PostHog to help Launchdesk understand feature usage and app reliability. Events may include app opens, local workflow usage, category filter usage, saved or opened candidate actions by source/category, app version, build number, operating system version, device family, anonymous install ID, and refresh timing.

Telemetry Limits

DealDesk does not send search text, email address, precise location, contacts, advertising identifiers, exact deal titles, or exact deal URLs in telemetry events.

Local Data

Saved candidates, shopping-list items, deal-check plans, compare sets, local deal drafts, hidden local deal preferences, and alert preferences remain on the user's device.

Affiliate Purchase Links

DealDesk may earn a commission when a user chooses to open some merchant purchase links. If affiliate links are configured, the destination merchant or affiliate network may receive standard click and purchase attribution data.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to hello.launchdesk@gmail.com.
